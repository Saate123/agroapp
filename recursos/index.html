Sobre Nós

AgroApp is the Super Agricultural App that contains everything you need to know about more than 15 crops, a complete phytosanitary registry with around 3535 pesticides with recommendations and leaflets, as well as Diseases, pests and weeds with updated photos in high definition.

Our Mission

AGROAPP

At AgroApp, our mission is to empower farmers and agricultural professionals with a dynamic and interactive platform to share knowledge, best practices, and innovative solutions. We strive to provide comprehensive and reliable information on crop management, pest control, and agricultural inputs, while fostering a community that promotes sustainable agriculture, collaboration, and mutual growth. By creating a platform that facilitates knowledge sharing, collaboration, and innovation, we aim to support farmers and agricultural professionals in their efforts to adopt sustainable practices, improve crop yields, and contribute to a more sustainable food system. Ultimately, our goal is to drive positive change in the agricultural industry and promote a more equitable and sustainable food future for all.

Our Vision

Empowering Sustainable Agriculture:

Providing knowledge, resources, and innovative solutions to promote sustainable agriculture practices, improve crop yields, and reduce waste.

Fostering a Collaborative Community:

Establishing a vibrant community of farmers, agricultural professionals, and industry experts who share knowledge, expertise, and resources to drive innovation and growth.

Harnessing Technology for Impact:

Leveraging emerging technologies like AI, blockchain, and IoT to enhance the AgroApp platform, improve data analysis, and provide more accurate insights to users.

Plantas Daninhas

O AgroApp tem soluções para combater mais de 40 ervas daninhas.

Ervas daninhas na área? Não se preocupe!

Para cada erva daninha registrada, você encontrará uma descrição completa da planta invasora, identificação, características de disseminação e propagação, bem como uma lista completa de herbicidas recomendados.
